Academics

St. K Michael's School is a reputable educational institution committed to delivering high-quality education. It fosters an inclusive and nurturing environment where students can grow academically, socially, and personally. The school emphasizes holistic development, equipping students with the skills needed for future success.

The St K Michael's School Difference

St K Michael's School has established itself as a beacon of academic excellence within the Okaikwei North Municipal Assembly. For over a decade, the school has consistently ranked among the top ten performers in the Basic Education Certificate Exams (BECE), a testament to the dedication of its educators and students alike. Furthermore, St K Michael's School proudly boasts a remarkable 100% pass rate in the BECE, demonstrating its commitment to ensuring that every student succeeds.

By fostering a culture of excellence and nurturing the potential of each learner, St K Michael's School continues to uphold its reputation as a leading institution in the region, preparing students not just for exams but for lifelong success.

Pre-School (Crèche-Kindergarten)

Pre-School

At St K Michael's School, our Pre-School program is designed for children aged 3 months to 5 years, focusing on their holistic development—social, physical, emotional, and intellectual. With well-equipped classrooms and a resourceful library, we nurture a love for learning from the earliest stages. Our vision is to create an environment where every child feels valued, respected, and encouraged to thrive. Our mission is to provide quality education and childcare in a safe, inclusive space that fosters positive early learning experiences essential for lifelong growth.

Lower Primary

The Lower Primary (classes 1-3) at St K Michael's School is committed to building strong foundations for academic and personal growth. Each classroom is equipped with modern, age-appropriate learning materials, and students have access to facilities such as a computer lab and library. Vision: To instill confidence and curiosity in young learners. Mission: To deliver a nurturing education that promotes intellectual, social, and emotional success.

Upper Primary

The Upper Primary (classes 4-6) prepares students for the transition to higher academic challenges. With advanced facilities like a comprehensive library and a technology-rich computer lab, we focus on fostering academic excellence and character development. Vision: To excel in both academics and values-based education. Mission: To provide a holistic educational experience that equips students with the tools to succeed academically, socially, and ethically.

Junior High School (JHS)

St K Michael's JHS department (Basic 7-9) is dedicated to preparing students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE). For the past decade, we have consistently ranked among the top ten performers in the Okaikwei North Municipal Assembly, achieving a 100% BECE pass rate. With a curriculum approved by the Ghana Education Service, students are guided by experienced and compassionate educators who mentor them both academically and personally. Our focus is on shaping well-rounded individuals ready for future academic and professional endeavors.

About us

Our Mission is to to be an institution of learning that nurtures and raises learners to fit in to society with strong academic and moral discipline.
